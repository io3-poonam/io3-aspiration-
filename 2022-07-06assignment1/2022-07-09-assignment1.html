Hello, my name is Doremon

It was started in a children's magazine in 1969. Doraemon is the name of a robot cat that came from the future to help a boy named Nobita Nobi. .

Details

Nobita Nobi is a ten-year-old around Japanese school boy, who is kind-hearted and honest, but also lazy, unlucky, weak, and bad grades and sports. One day, a robot cat from the 22nd century named Doraemon was sent back to the past by Nobita's descendants to take care of Nobita, so that his descendants can get a better life. Then Doraemon became his best friend and family.
